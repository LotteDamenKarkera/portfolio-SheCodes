About me

Front-end developer from Amsterdam, The Netherlands

Hi, my name is Lotte-Damen Karkera, a 27-year old mama living in Amsterdam with my amazing husband, powerful daughter and energetic dog. As a graduated dietitian now working as a Product Developer I am passionate about (low-carb) nutrition, yoga, running, strength training, positive mindset work and - maybe surprisingly - digital design and coding. When I was a kid from 12 years, I spent hours and hours creating websites by self-taught HTML, CSS, Javascript and Adobe Photoshop skills. Unfortunately due to lack of time and fellow website owners who quit - this hobby faded away. It is said that the thing you really enjoyed doing as a kid is your true calling. That's why I have decided to reignite this fire!
